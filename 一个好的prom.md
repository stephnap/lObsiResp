1
被低估股票筛选器 (Undervalued Stock Screener) 不要听大 V 喊单，让数据说话。这个指令能帮你找出基本面强劲但价格被错杀的公司。
Prompt:"Analyze the current stock market and identify 10 undervalued companies with strong fundamentals. Filter for: P/E ratio below industry average, positive earnings growth, low debt-to-equity ratio, and analyst ratings showing potential upside of 30%+." (中文：分析当前股市，找出10家基本面强劲但被低估的公司。筛选条件：市盈率低于行业平均水平、正盈利增长、低债务权益比，且分析师评级显示有30%以上的上涨潜力。)

2
内部交易模式分析 (Insider Trading Pattern Analyzer) 春江水暖鸭先知。高管敢花真金白银买自家股票，是信心最强的信号。
Prompt:"Review recent insider trading activity for [sector/industry]. Identify companies where executives are buying significant amounts of their own stock. What does this signal about company confidence? Give me the top 5 with the strongest insider buying patterns." (中文：回顾[某行业]近期的内部交易活动。识别出高管正在大量买入自家股票的公司。这释放了什么信心信号？给我内部买入模式最强的前5名。)

3
市场情绪 vs 现实差距 (Market Sentiment vs Reality Gap) 别人恐慌我贪婪。这个指令专门寻找那些“被情绪错杀”的好公司。
Prompt:"Find stocks that are currently oversold due to negative sentiment but have strong underlying fundamentals. Compare market perception versus actual financial health. Which companies are being unfairly punished by the market right now?" (中文：寻找那些因负面市场情绪被超卖，但底层基本面依然强劲的股票。对比市场认知与实际财务健康状况。目前哪些公司正被市场不公平地惩罚？)


4
股息贵族 ROI 计算器 (Dividend Aristocrat ROI Calculator) 适合稳健派。
不仅看股息率，还要看“可持续性”。
Prompt:"Analyze dividend aristocrats (companies with 25+ years of consistent dividend increases). Calculate the total return including dividend reinvestment over the past 10 years. Rank them by yield, payout ratio sustainability, and dividend growth rate." (中文：分析股息贵族（连续25年增加股息的公司）。计算过去10年包含股息再投资的总回报。按收益率、派息比率可持续性和股息增长率进行排名。)

5
科技股炒作 vs 基本面 (Tech Stock Hype vs Fundamentals) 谁在裸泳？这个指令帮你剥离 Hype，看到真实的营收和现金流。
Prompt:"Compare the current valuations of top tech stocks against their revenue growth, profit margins, and cash flow. Which are overvalued based on hype? Which are undervalued despite solid fundamentals? Give me 3 overpriced and 3 underpriced picks." (中文：对比顶级科技股的当前估值与其营收增长、利润率和现金流。哪些是基于炒作的高估？哪些是基本面稳健但被低估？给我3个高估和3个低估的选择。)

6
板块轮动信号检测 (Sector Rotation Signal Detector) 不要在逆风的时候扬帆。让 AI 告诉你资金正在流向哪个板块。
Prompt:"Analyze macroeconomic indicators (interest rates, inflation, GDP growth) and predict which market sectors will outperform in the next 6-12 months. Based on current economic conditions, which sectors should I rotate into?" (中文：分析宏观经济指标（利率、通胀、GDP增长）并预测未来6-12个月哪些板块表现优异。基于当前经济状况，我应该轮动到哪些板块？)

7
小盘成长股挖掘 (Small Cap Growth Identifier) 寻找下一个百倍股。专注于那些机构还没大量持仓的“隐形宝石”。
Prompt:"Identify small-cap stocks (under $2B market cap) with high growth potential. Filter for: revenue growth above 20% annually, expanding profit margins, strong balance sheets, and low institutional ownership. Give me 5 hidden gems." (中文：识别具有高增长潜力的小盘股（市值低于20亿美元）。筛选条件：年营收增长超过20%、利润率扩张、资产负债表强劲且机构持股比例低。给我5个被忽视的宝石。)

8
风险调整回报优化 (Risk-Adjusted Return Optimizer) 这一步最关键。不仅仅是买什么，而是买多少。
Prompt:"I have a $50,000 portfolio with [risk tolerance: conservative/moderate/aggressive]. Build a diversified portfolio across sectors with optimal risk-adjusted returns. Include position sizing, expected returns, and rebalancing strategy." (中文：我有$50,000投资组合，风险承受能力为[保守/适中/激进]。构建一个跨板块的多元化投资组合，以此获得最佳的风险调整回报。包括仓位大小、预期回报和再平衡策略。)
